1. Overview
This document summarizes key contractual terms for active suppliers and vendors, including notice periods, termination clauses, payment terms, services provided, and contact details.

2. Supplier Contracts

2.1 Supplier: WorkLink HR Systems Ltd
Service Provided: Cloud-based HR system
Contract Start Date: 01 Jan 2024
Contract End Date: 31 Dec 2026
Renewal Term: Auto-renewal annually unless terminated

Notice Period:
90 days before contract end date

Early Termination Clause:
Can terminate with 60 days' notice and payment of early termination fee equal to 2 months of service

Payment Terms:
Net 30 days

Support SLAs:
- Critical issues: 4 hours
- Standard issues: 2 business days

Data Ownership:
All HR data remains with client; export available within 15 days of termination

2.2 Supplier: TalentWave Recruitment Agency
Service Provided: Recruitment services (perm & temp)
Contract Start Date: 10 Feb 2023
Contract Duration: Ongoing, reviewed annually

Notice Period:
30 days written notice by either party

Early Termination Clause:
No termination fee; standard notice applies

Placement Fee:
15% of candidate’s first-year salary

Refund Clause:
50% rebate if candidate leaves within 3 months

Payment Terms:
Net 15 days; 10% discount for payment within 5 days

2.3 Supplier: OfficeXpress Ltd
Service Provided: Office supplies
Contract Start Date: 01 Mar 2022
Contract End Date: 28 Feb 2025

Notice Period:
60 days before contract end

Early Termination Clause:
Can exit early with 45 days’ notice and payment of remaining minimum annual spend (£2,000)

Payment Terms:
Net 30 days; 2% interest on late payments

2.4 Supplier: CyberSafe IT Security Ltd
Service Provided: Cybersecurity monitoring
Contract Start Date: 15 May 2024
Contract Duration: 2 years + 1-year optional extension

Notice Period:
90 days before renewal

Early Termination Clause:
30 days’ notice allowed without fee for SLA breach; otherwise, 3-month service fee payable

Payment Terms:
Quarterly in advance

Compliance Obligations:
Must meet ISO 27001; provide quarterly audit reports
