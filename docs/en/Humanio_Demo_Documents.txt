Hybrid Working Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Office attendance is
required for key meetings, collaboration sessions, and monthly team
days.

Maternity Policy

Eligible employees receive 52 weeks of maternity leave: 26 weeks at
enhanced pay (12 weeks full pay, 14 weeks half pay) followed by 26 weeks
statutory. Notification must be provided 15 weeks before the expected
due date.

Expenses Policy

Employees may claim expenses related to travel, accommodation, meals,
and work-related purchases. Claims must be submitted within 30 days
using SAP Concur with receipts attached. Claims are reimbursed within
the next payroll cycle.

Employee Benefits

Employees are entitled to private medical insurance, pension
contributions at 6%, 25 days annual leave plus bank holidays, wellbeing
allowance, and access to the Employee Assistance Programme.

ATS Contract Exit Clause

The contract requires 90 days written notice. Early termination within
the first 12 months incurs a fee equal to 25% of the remaining annual
subscription.

Payroll System Contract Payment Terms

Invoices are issued quarterly in advance with 30-day payment terms. Late
payments may incur a 2% service fee.

HRIS Early Termination

Leaving the HRIS contract early may incur an early-exit fee calculated
as 30% of the remaining contract value.

Resignation Process

1.  Employee submits resignation in writing.
2.  Manager acknowledges and informs HR.
3.  HR processes termination in the HRIS.
4.  Exit interview scheduled.
5.  Final payroll and equipment return completed.

Grievance Process

1.  Employee raises grievance in writing to HR.
2.  HR assigns an investigator.
3.  Meetings held within 5 working days.
4.  Outcome communicated in writing.
5.  Employee may appeal within 7 days.

Recruitment Process

1.  Hiring manager completes recruitment request form.
2.  HR approves via workflow and posts vacancy.
3.  Screening and interviews conducted.
4.  Offer approval submitted to HR.
5.  Candidate onboarded.

SAP Concur – Submitting an Expense

1.  Log in to SAP Concur.
2.  Click “Create Expense Report.”
3.  Add expense types, receipts, and details.
4.  Submit for approval.

Workday – Delegating Workflows

1.  Navigate to Profile → Actions.
2.  Select “Manage Delegations.”
3.  Add a delegate and date range.
4.  Submit.

Workday – Requesting Feedback

1.  Go to “Feedback” in Workday.
2.  Select “Request Feedback.”
3.  Choose recipients and send request.

First Day Information

Employees should arrive at 9:00 AM at the main reception. Ask for the HR
Coordinator who will provide induction materials and access.

Dress Code

Smart casual is expected in the office. Client-facing meetings may
require business attire.

Probation Length

Standard probation period is 6 months, with review at 3 and 6 months.
